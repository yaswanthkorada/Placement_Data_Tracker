Placements Data Tracker

creating a master database on campus placements could be a valuable tool for addressing the issue of unemployability in the country. It would provide policy makers with the information they need to develop targeted interventions and policies to improve employment outcomes for graduates of technical institutes and universities.

In addition, there may be challenges in ensuring the accuracy and completeness of the data.

Some educational institutions and employers may be reluctant to share information, or may not have the resources to collect and report data on campus placements.

A placement tracking website could be a useful tool for tracking the placement details of technical institutes and universities throughout the country. Such a website could collect information on campus placements from educational institutions and employers, and make this information available to students, job seekers, and policy makers.
